About Me

My name is Brandon Nelson. Welcome to my website. I earned a BA degree in English from Central College in Pella, IA. I've had a long career in outside sales and, most recently, operations management. With a passion for problem-solving, I decided to learn web development. I'm currently enrolled in the University of Denver Full-Stack Web Development bootcamp.

In my downtime, I enjoy spending time with my wife and three kids; listening to mystery and self-development audio books; weight training, with fast walking mixed in; and making my yard look great. Patience, accuracy, and productivity are all things I value when working with teammates or on my own. Thanks for exploring my portfolio site!
