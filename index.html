About Me

I'm Brandon Nelson, and welcome to my portfolio website. I earned a BA degree in English from Central College in Pella, IA. Even though I'm a storyteller and avid reader of Stephen King, Gregg Hurwitz, and James Lee Burke, I am good at business documentation. I am a Technical Writer. I earned a certificate in Full-Stack Web Development through the University of Denver in June of 2020.

This website is designed to tell you a little about me and my accomplishments so far, as well as give you ways of reaching out to me. I'm interested in web development and technical writing opportunities. I bring research expertise, an understanding of working with GitHub, HTML/CSS/JavaScript/jQuery application, interpersonal communication, and leadership skills to your projects. Always moving in a positive direction and being a great teammate are professional (and personal!) values of mine.

In my downtime, I enjoy hiking and debating with my wife and three kids. I exercise routinely, and I care about making this planet a better place for us all. Patience, accuracy, and productivity are important values to me. Thanks for exploring my portfolio site!
